To switch views between Items and Services, simply click on either Items or Services in the main section.

To view the full Service details, simply tap on the service (or any service, or area on the services section) and click the Expanded Service View button, which is the button atop the list. To hide the services details for easier scrolling, simply click on the Retracted View button, which is the button in the middle of the array of the pop-up buttons. To hide the pop-up, simply click on the close (X) button.

To upload items and services, as well as access your personal information, simply click on the menu icon in the top right part of your screen, and then click on the large, round (User) icon.

To view your log status, simply click on the colored, small, round (User) icon in the top left part of your screen.

To sign up or log in, click on the menu icon then click on the User icon. You will be redirected to the sign in/up page if you're not logged in.
